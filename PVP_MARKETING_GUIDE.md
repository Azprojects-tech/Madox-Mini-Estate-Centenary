## 🔧 Portal Features Explained

### **Interactive Map Controls:**
- **🌍 Land Button**: Hides the green parcel overlays so clients can see the actual satellite view of the land clearly
- **🔢 Numbers Button**: Toggles parcel numbers on/off - useful when client wants clean view without number labels cluttering the map
- **🔍 Zoom Controls**: Let users zoom in/out to see individual parcels or entire estate layout

### **Parcel Status System:**
- **Green**: Available for sale
- **Red**: Sold (buyer identified and paid)
- **Yellow**: Reserved (buyer showing serious interest, deposit paid)
- **Blue**: Under development/special status

### **Dashboard Cards (8-Card System):**
1. **Total Revenue**: Shows all money generated from estate
2. **Total Parcels**: Number of plots in the entire estate  
3. **Sold**: Parcels with completed sales
4. **Reserved**: Parcels with deposits/commitments
5. **Available**: Open parcels ready for sale
6. **Expected Revenue**: Projected income from reserved parcels
7. **Occupancy Rate**: Percentage of estate that's sold/reserved
8. **Average Price**: Mean price per parcel
